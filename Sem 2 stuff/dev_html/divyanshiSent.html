Medicines


For Pain & Fever
  1. Paracetamol 350mg/650mg
      2. Uses
      • For normal pain in body
      • For fever
      Doses
      • After Breakfast
      • After Lunch
      • Before Bed

    Before Using

    350mg
    Prefered for kids [above 8yrs and below 16yrs]

    Can be given 3 times a day iff given to a adult

    650mg
    Prefered for adults [above 16yrs]

    Should not be given more than 2 times even to adults


  2. Disprine
      3. Uses
      • For severe pain in body
      • For fever
      Doses
      • Only once a day

    Before Using

    WARNING
    Should not be given without prescription

    Overdosing might cause liver failure or other organ failure
